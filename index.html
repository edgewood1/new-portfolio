Who Am I?

I'm Mel DeJesus. I live in Durham, NC. As a youngster, I had a fondness for an early form of the BASIC computer language.

In a roundabout way, this led me briefly through an Ohio engineering program before switching to literature and philosophy, both of which I still have a deep affinity for.

Later, I spent time teaching classes in writing and research at various NC colleges, exploring the ways that society and media inform, persuade and motivate us.

Curious about other forms of landscape, I also spent time at an eco-contemplative center where I worked as an outdoor guide and at a wildcrafting & outdoor school where I facilitated its sales, operational, and communications strategies.

Presently, I've returned to writing code, exploring software and front-end web development. I appreciate the way coding allows me to integrate many of my past interests while exploring new forms of expression.
